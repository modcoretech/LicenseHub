License Chooser Help

Answer a few questions to help us recommend the best license for your project.

1. What type of project are you licensing?

2. Do you want to allow commercial use of your project?

3. Do you want to require attribution (credit) for your work?

4. Do you want modifications/derivatives to be distributed under the same license? (Share-alike)

5. For software, do you want to explicitly grant patent rights from contributors to users?

6. Is your software primarily a library that others might link to in their (possibly proprietary) applications?

7. For creative content, do you want to allow adaptations or modifications of your work?
